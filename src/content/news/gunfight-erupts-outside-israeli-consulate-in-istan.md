A security incident has unfolded outside the Israeli Consulate in Istanbul after three armed individuals opened fire on police personnel guarding the diplomatic mission. The ensuing exchange of fire resulted in one of the attackers being neutralised on the scene, according to local reports.

Turkish security forces have cordoned off the surrounding area and launched an extensive investigation into the motives behind the broad-daylight confrontation. The diplomatic facility remains under heavy guard as authorities assess the potential for further threats to international personnel.

> "This breach of diplomatic sanctuary signals a worrying intensification of regional tensions manifesting on the streets of global hubs."

Global security agencies have been on high alert following increased threats to diplomatic outposts in recent months. Istanbul officials are working closely with international counterparts to determine if the assailants were acting alone or as part of a larger coordinated effort.
