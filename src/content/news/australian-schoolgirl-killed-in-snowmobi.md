A young Australian schoolgirl has tragically died following a snowmobile accident during a family holiday in Japan. Eight-year-old Chloe Jeffries was riding the vehicle with her mother when it reportedly flipped, leaving the young girl trapped underneath. 

Local authorities and emergency services attended the scene, but despite their efforts, Chloe could not be saved. The Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade (DFAT) has confirmed it is providing urgent consular assistance to the Jeffries family as they navigate this devastating loss while overseas.

Tributes have begun to pour in for the primary school student, with her local community back home left in shock by the sudden tragedy. Investigations into the exact circumstances surrounding the crash are currently underway as the family prepares for the difficult process of bringing Chloe home.
