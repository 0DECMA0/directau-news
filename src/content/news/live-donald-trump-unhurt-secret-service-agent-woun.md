Former President Donald Trump has emerged unscathed following a high-stakes security breach at the White House Correspondents’ Dinner in Washington, D.C., which resulted in the wounding of a Secret Service agent. The incident, which occurred during one of the most high-profile social fixtures on the American political calendar, prompted an immediate and aggressive response from federal tactical units.

A male suspect, reportedly carrying multiple weapons, was apprehended after attempting to charge a primary security checkpoint at the venue. The ensuing confrontation necessitated an immediate lockdown of the premises, though officials confirm that Mr Trump was moved to a secure location and suffered no physical harm during the chaos.
