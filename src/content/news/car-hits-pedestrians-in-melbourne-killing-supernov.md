A young man returning from a pop culture convention in Melbourne’s north-west has lost his life after a vehicle struck a group of pedestrians on Saturday evening. Victoria Police confirmed the victim was an attendee of the Supernova Comic Con & Gaming event, marking a devastating end to what should have been a day of celebration for the local community.

Emergency services were called to the Ascot Vale area following reports of a significant collision involving multiple individuals. While the Major Collision Investigation Unit continues to establish the exact timeline of events, authorities are scrutinising the driver’s actions and the environmental conditions that preceded this fatal encounter on our city's streets.

> "This incident serves as a grim reminder of the fragility of our shared public spaces, where a moment of joy can be instantly eclipsed by an unforeseen tragedy."

Support services have been extended to the victims' families and the broader pop culture community as the investigation remains active. Detectives are urging any witnesses or individuals with dashcam footage of the incident to contact Crime Stoppers immediately to assist in the ongoing inquiry.
