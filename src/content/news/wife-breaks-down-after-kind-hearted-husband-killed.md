Victoria Police have launched a major homicide investigation following the discovery of 65-year-old Richard Willis on his rural property in the state’s north-west. Detectives confirmed the local farmer was located in a shallow grave on his farm after being reported missing, marking a grim conclusion to the search for the well-known local.

The community has been left profoundly shaken by the loss of a man described by neighbours as 'kind-hearted' and 'selfless.' As forensic teams continue to process the site, Mr Willis’s widow has expressed her immense grief over the sudden and violent loss that has shattered their quiet life on the land.

> "This tragedy represents a profound breach of the peace in our rural heartlands, reminding us that even the most secluded sanctuaries are not immune to the shadows of human malice."

As the investigation enters a critical phase, authorities are appealing for any witnesses or individuals with dashcam footage from the area to come forward. No suspects are currently in custody, but detectives are working around the clock to ensure justice for a man who was a pillar of his local community.
