Victoria Police detectives have intercepted and charged a 67-year-old man at Melbourne International Airport as he allegedly attempted to board an overseas flight. The arrest follows the grim discovery of a woman's body in Victoria’s south-west on Friday, bringing a tragic end to a missing person’s search that had commenced earlier that week.

The investigation moved with significant pace after the victim was reported missing on Thursday, leading authorities to track the suspect to the departure gates before he could leave Australian jurisdiction. Forensic specialists remain at the regional site where the remains were located, as Homicide Squad detectives work to establish the precise timeline of events.

> "The intersection of a local tragedy and a desperate attempt at international flight underscores the gravity of the legal process now underway."

The accused has been remanded in custody and is scheduled to appear before the Melbourne Magistrates' Court. Police have indicated that the parties were known to each other, though the identity of the deceased is currently being withheld while extended family members are notified of the development.
