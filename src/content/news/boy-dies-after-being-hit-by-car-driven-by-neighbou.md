A community in Melbourne’s outer suburbs has been plunged into mourning following the tragic death of a seven-year-old boy who was struck by a vehicle while riding his scooter on Thursday afternoon. Victoria Police and emergency services were called to the quiet residential street, but despite the desperate efforts of first responders, the child succumbed to his injuries at the scene.

Preliminary investigations suggest the driver involved was a neighbour of the young boy’s family. The incident occurred just metres from the child’s front door, turning a routine afternoon of play into a scene of profound devastation. Major Collision Investigation Unit detectives spent several hours on-site cordoning off the area and documenting the circumstances of the collision.

> "The fragility of suburban peace is never more apparent than when a moment of everyday play ends in an unthinkable tragedy that alters the fabric of a community forever."

The driver of the vehicle stopped immediately and is currently assisting police with their ongoing enquiries. As is standard procedure in such incidents, no charges have been laid at this early stage while officers continue to piece together the sequence of events. Authorities have made a public appeal for anyone with dashcam footage or further information to contact Crime Stoppers as soon as possible.
