Victoria Police are continuing their search for the driver of a vehicle involved in a devastating hit-run in Melbourne’s suburbs, which has left a father of four fighting for his life. The incident occurred late Saturday evening as the family was departing an engagement party, transforming a milestone celebration into a scene of absolute chaos in a matter of seconds.

The victim’s wife, speaking exclusively from the hospital where her husband remains in a critical state, has issued a harrowing plea for the culprits to surrender to authorities. Investigators have established a crime scene and are currently reviewing CCTV footage from surrounding properties to identify the dark-coloured vehicle seen fleeing the area shortly after the impact without stopping to render assistance.

> "The integrity of our social fabric is tested when the duty of care on our roads is abandoned, leaving a family to navigate the wreckage of a life-altering moment alone."

As the local community rallies around the affected family, the incident has renewed calls for increased road safety measures and stricter penalties for those who evade responsibility. Authorities urge anyone with dashcam footage or information regarding the vehicle’s whereabouts to contact Crime Stoppers immediately to assist in the ongoing investigation.
