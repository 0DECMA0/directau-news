Northern Territory Police have launched a formal investigation following the tragic discovery of an infant found deceased inside a vehicle in the Darwin satellite city of Palmerston on Friday. Emergency services were called to a suburban residence where the child was located within a car parked on a private driveway.

Major Crime Squad detectives are currently forensically examining the scene to establish the exact timeline of events leading up to the discovery. While the specific circumstances remain under investigation, authorities have confirmed that a report will be prepared for the Coroner as they work to determine the cause of the passing.

> "In the face of such an unfathomable loss, the collective weight of a community's grief serves as a somber reminder of our shared responsibility to protect the most vulnerable."

Police have urged the public to respect the privacy of the grieving family as they navigate this traumatic period. Local support services remain on standby for those affected by the incident, and anyone with relevant information is encouraged to contact the Northern Territory Police immediately.
