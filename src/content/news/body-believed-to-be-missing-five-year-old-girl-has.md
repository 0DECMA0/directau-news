Northern Territory Police have located a body believed to be that of a five-year-old girl who vanished from her Alice Springs home earlier this week. The discovery was made during an intensive search operation involving specialist units and local volunteers, ending a period of agonising uncertainty for the community.

While formal identification is yet to be completed, authorities have cordoned off the scene to allow forensic investigators to conduct a thorough examination of the area. Senior officers have praised the tireless efforts of those involved in the search while acknowledging the profound grief now gripping the Red Centre.

> "In the face of such a devastating outcome, the collective heart of the nation breaks for a family enduring every parent's most harrowing nightmare."

A report is being prepared for the Coroner as detectives continue to piece together the events leading up to the child's disappearance. Officials have requested that the public respect the family's privacy during this deeply distressing time as the investigation transitions into its next phase.
