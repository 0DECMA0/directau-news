NSW Police have launched a critical investigation after a man succumbed to his injuries following a physical altercation in Sydney’s western suburbs on Tuesday afternoon.

Emergency services were called to the scene following reports of a serious incident, with social media footage depicting a massive response including more than a dozen patrol cars, multiple ambulances, and the PolAir helicopter hovering overhead.

> "This tragic escalation of street-level conflict represents a sobering reminder of the fragility of suburban peace and the relentless pressure placed upon our frontline responders."

A crime scene has been established and forensic specialists are currently canvassing the area for evidence. Authorities are appealing for anyone with information or dashcam footage to contact Crime Stoppers as they work to determine the circumstances surrounding this fatal encounter.
