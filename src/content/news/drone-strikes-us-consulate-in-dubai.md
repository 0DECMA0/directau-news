The deteriorating situation has left thousands of Australians stranded and anxious, with many desperately seeking a way to flee the growing conflict. As commercial flight options become increasingly limited and the security environment shifts rapidly, families back home are calling for urgent government assistance to ensure the safe passage of those caught in the crisis.
